Random Nutrition Privacy Policy

Effective Date: Tuesday, April 1, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to the Random Nutrition Privacy Policy. This policy explains how we, Killian Cengo ("we," "our," "us"), collect, use, and protect the information you ("user," "you," "your") provide or that is generated when you use our mobile application "Random Nutrition" (the "App"). Your privacy is a priority for us, and we are committed to being transparent about our data collection and management practices.

Information We Collect

We collect two main categories of information when you use the Random Nutrition App:

User-Provided Data:

  • The foods you record in the App.
  • Macronutrient information (e.g., protein, carbohydrates, fat) that you provide to customize your meals or track your eating habits.
  • This data is collected to improve the App and is sent to our servers.

Automatically Collected Data:

  • Usage statistics: For example, which features you use the most or how frequently you use the App.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system (iOS, Android, etc.), and unique identifiers (such as the device ID).
  • Diagnostics: Data about the App’s performance, such as crash reports or technical errors.
  • This data is collected to improve the App and to fix technical issues.

How We Collect Information

User-Provided Data: You enter this information directly through the App interface, for example by adding foods or entering macronutrient data.

Automatically Collected Data: We use technologies such as software development kits (SDKs) for analytics and crash reporting to collect this information in the background while you use the App.

How We Use the Information

We use the information collected for the following purposes:

  • To provide and personalize the App: For example, generating balanced meals based on the data you provide.
  • To improve the App: Analyzing usage statistics to identify which features to optimize or bugs to fix.
  • To develop new features: Using the data to design enhancements or new services tailored to your needs.
  • To diagnose technical problems: Using crash reports and performance data to ensure a smooth and stable user experience.

Information Sharing

User-Provided Data: This data (recorded foods and macronutrient information) is not shared with third parties and is only transmitted to our servers.

Automatically Collected Data: We collect this data on our servers exclusively for analysis purposes. It is not shared with third parties.

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. This includes the use of standard security technologies and limiting access to data only to those who need it to provide our services. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

User Rights

You have rights over your data according to applicable privacy laws (such as the GDPR in Europe or the CCPA in California):

  • User-Provided Data: You have full control over this information. You can view, edit, or delete it at any time directly through the App interface.
  • Automatically Collected Data: You may have the right to access, correct, or delete this data. To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@randomnutrition.fr. We will respond to your request within the timeframes required by law.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 4. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children in this age group. If we discover that personal data of a child under 4 has been collected without verifiable parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information as soon as possible.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technological developments, or legal requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page with a new effective date. We encourage you to review this policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your data.

Contact

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this privacy policy or our data practices, please contact us at: support@randomnutrition.fr. We are committed to responding to your requests within a maximum of 3 business days.
