Overview of Random Nutrition

Random Nutrition is a mobile application that generates balanced meals randomly while meeting predefined nutritional constraints. The app produces appetizers, main courses, cheeses, and desserts, taking into account energy, protein, fat, carbohydrate, and other macronutrient needs.

It also offers the option to add or remove certain types of foods to personalize meals according to your preferences and requirements.

Key Features

  • Random meal generation that adheres to nutritional limits for each course (appetizer, main course, cheese, dessert).
  • Customization of nutritional limits (energy, macronutrient ratios).
  • Management of food quantities within predefined ranges.
  • Automatic verification of nutritional constraints for each generated meal.
  • Compliance with dietary guidelines: every meal is balanced and meets daily energy, protein, fat, and carbohydrate requirements.

App Pages

Home Page

Users can view their usage statistics and discover some random foods.

Food Page

This page displays two lists:

  • The default food list.
  • The customizable food list that users can add to.

By clicking the + button, users are redirected to a page for creating a custom food item that includes a name, categories, nutritional values, a description, etc. Each food item in the list is clickable to display a popup with detailed information.

Generate Meals Page

On this page, users can:

  • Enter the desired nutritional values.
  • Select the number of meals to generate.
  • Individually configure each meal by selecting desired courses and categories or foods.
  • Generate the meals by clicking the "Generate" button after configuration.

A loading screen appears with a tip, and once finished, the generated meals are displayed in detail with the option to save them individually or as a set.

Meals & Meal Sets Page

This page presents two lists:

  • Saved meals.
  • Saved meal sets.
